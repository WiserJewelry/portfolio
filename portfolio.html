About Me

Wiser is a jewelry designer who blends HipHop aesthetic with gothic undertones. My work is inspired by the outrageous chains, rings and pendants of the rap industry, and emo/punk fashion. Each piece is designed in a variety of CAD programs and are fabricated by me.

Not Dead Yet (NDY) is a pop-punk, emo, rap inspired branding under the Wiser Jewelry umbrella that seeks to embody an expression of identity, rebellion, and resilience. NDY captures underground energy in bold, experimental pieces that speak for those still fighting, and evolving. It’s jewelry with a pulse.
